About NestQuest

NestQuest is a smart roommate compatibility finder. It helps you find the perfect roommate based on shared lifestyles to ensure a peaceful living environment.

🚀 How It Works

  • Create your personal profile
  • Define your roommate criteria
  • Swipe through potential matches
  • Connect via automated email

⚙️ The Algorithm

Each match gives 25% compatibility:

  • 🌙 Sleep Schedules
  • 🧹 Cleanliness Standards
  • 📚 Study Environment
  • 🤝 Social Energy
